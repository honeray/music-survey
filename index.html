Study Debrief

This was a study to determine how non-aural context — information about a piece's background, composer, or origin — can affect human perception of music quality.

Important disclosure: All of the songs you heard were actually human-made. Any context stating that a song was AI-generated was fictitious and included intentionally as part of the study design to examine how such labels affect perceived quality.

We hope this offers an interesting perspective on the way we all experience music.
